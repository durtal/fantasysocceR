gameweeks

The gameweeks dataset contains data for each of the gameweeks in the fantasy.premierleague.com game. It is likely that gameweeks change as fixtures are cancelled and moved.

Once the package has been loaded, to access the dataset:

data(gameweeks)

The dataframe has dimensions 760 rows and 5 columns.

str(gameweeks)
